Logo Guidelines

Brand Logo

Get to know EZShield's official brand logo and how to use it accurately. Follow the sizing, space and placement guidelines below to ensure a clear, high-quality image, avoid distortion of the image, maintain excellent contrast between the image and its background, and retain appropriate image resolution.

Official Logo

Complete brand name in full color.

Download .png for web Download .pdf for print

Clear Space

The minimum clear space around the logo should be 15px for optimum visibility.

Minimum Size

The minimum width of the EZShield logo is 125px to ensure a crisp legible image.

Logo Variations

Instances in which is not feasible to use our official full-color logo, these variations may be used.

Partner-Integrated Logos

These are the logo options available to represent partnerships with EZShield.

EZShield Partnering with Partner X

For website headers/email headers/marketing materials: the space between the EZShield official logo and the partner name should be 4 percent of the width of two logos together.

Partner X Partnering with EZShield

For website headers/email headers/marketing materials: the space between the Partner logo and "Partnering with EZShield Fraud Protection" should be 4 percent of the width of logo and text together.

Partner X Powered by EZShield

For website headers/email headers/marketing materials: the space between the Partner logo and "Powered by EZShield Fraud Protection" should be 4 percent of the width of logo and text together.

Powered by EZShield Logo – Full Color

For website/email/marketing footers, the full color Powered by EZShield logo should be used.

Powered by EZShield Logo – Black & White
