Partner Marketing - Introduction

EZShield’s Adoption Marketing Campaign gives our partners the freedom to customize a marketing strategy that best fits their unique business goals and needs. EZShield offers multi-channel solutions to help promote identity crime awareness for our partners’ ID Protection programs both online and within their organization.

Adoption Marketing Program Overview

Customize how your organization markets your ID Protection program. Choose from our eight themes – whose messages focus on a variety of generational audiences – based on your unique target audience.

Choose from print and digital display options that best fit your organization’s chosen marketing strategy to be used in your own marketing campaigns.

Step 1: Pick Your Theme

Every business is different, but the concept of marketing remains the same: tailored solutions for your audience so that you can better cater to their specific needs. We offer eight themes that speak to various generational groups (i.e. Baby Boomers, Generation X, Millennials, etc.) so you can choose a theme to best fit your program.


Theme 1: Palm of Your Hand

Message

“Identity Protection in the Palm of Your Hand"


Goal

If the majority of your customers are using smartphones, tablets and other digital platforms on a day-to-day basis, they’re used to the convenience of having everything they need no matter where they go. What can you do to account for your customers’ digital shift? Shift with them.


“Palm of Your Hand” illustrates how your ID Protection program is easily accessible no matter where you go, emphasizing that our services are both secure and convenient for your customers’ busy lives.


Target

Theme 2: Open to Thieves

Message

“You Wouldn’t Leave Your Life Open to Thieves. Why Leave Your Identity at RISK?”


Goal

Would you feel comfortable leaving your front door unlocked while you were away? What about leaving expensive jewelry in plain sight, or keeping your car unlocked overnight? Even though we go out of our way to secure our valuables every day, most of your customers would not include their identities to that list.


“Open to Thieves” is a reminder that your most valuable asset is your identity. Your ID Protection program offers your customers all the tools they need to keep their identities and personal information secure – just as they would for their houses, cars and other valuable items.


Target

Theme 3: 10 Years of Change

Message

“A lot can change in 10 years. Discover Identity Protection Built for what’s ahead.”


Goal

Whether your customers are reminiscing on the past, or looking forward into the future, a lot can change over time. With new technological changes, devices and apps coming out every day, your customers may not always stop to think about the new risks posed to their identities.


Your ID Protection program is “built for what’s ahead,” designed to keep up with our ever-changing technological world. Offer your customers the protection they need against new ways fraudsters are committing fraud, identity theft and other identity crimes.


Target

Theme 4: How ID Thieves See You

Message

“How do identity thieves see you?”


Goal

The Dark Web is a booming marketplace for identity thieves. One way that fraudsters make money is by trading stolen or breached information on the online black market.


With data breaches and scams only continuing to increase, stolen information is sold on the Dark Web every day. Your ID Protection program gives your customers the tools they need to combat various online identity threats, as well as help prevent their identities from falling into the wrong hands.


Target

Theme 5: Protecting Your Story

Message

“Your name tells a story. Make sure it’s your own.”


Goal

Our names carry our stories and experiences, built from our valued accomplishments, hard work and prized memories. But identity thieves know that Shakespeare once said: “A rose by any other name would smell as sweet” – and they have no problem reaping the benefits of another’s good name.


“Protecting Your Story” is a reminder of the effort your customers put into creating their unique legacies. The services in your ID Protection program will help them secure what matters most, and help prevent criminals from rewriting their story.


Target

Theme 6: Looking Out For You

Message

“You’re always looking our for others, but who’s looking out for you?”


Goal

You are focused on your kids and grandkids and giving them the best of your time and energy. You need someone who is looking out for you in these changing times with Identity Protection.


Target

Theme 7: Me and My Identity

Message

“I am connected, adventurous and spontaneous. But I make sure my identity isn’t.”


Goal

You might be older now, but you are more connected and adventurous than ever. You need us to care about securing your identity so you can focus on living your life to the fullest.


Target

Theme 8: Connected World

Message

“Protect your identity in today’s connected world.”


Goal

Technology is changing fast; and all of your friends enjoy using it to connect with each other and your family. But as technology changes, so do risks, so protect your identity with business you can trust.


Target

Step 3: Pick Your Educational Resources & Tools

We provide several additional resources that act as educational tools to promote your program. Here are some ways our additional resources can further support your marketing efforts:

  • Use our guide on harmful and misleading language to be sure your messaging is accurate and effective.

  • Place the Live Fraud Feed on your website to keep your customers or employees up-to-date on the latest ID protection tips, breach and scam news updates.

  • Educational content on fraud, identity theft and other identity crime risks by providing informational data sheets and videos.

Educational Data Sheets

Our goal is to empower others to proactively secure their sensitive information and overall identities. Place any of these educational data sheets in your organization or as downloadables on your website to offer valuable information and tips on various types of identity theft, scams and other identity crimes.

Social Media Safety

Learn about the risks posed to your identity when using social media platforms, and how to properly secure your information on these social sites.

Child Identity Theft

Did you know that children can be victims of identity theft too? Learn why children are at risk of identity theft, and how to protect your child’s information from identity thieves.

Criminal Identity Theft

Sometimes fraudsters continue committing crimes past the initial theft of your identity. Learn how identity thieves can use your information to rack up other criminal charges – and leave you to deal with the mess.

Synthetic Identity Theft

Find out what makes synthetic identity theft so difficult to catch, and how to spot the signs before it becomes a bigger issue.

Medical Identity Theft

Your identity can be used for more than just stealing funds. Learn how criminals use your identity to acquire medical prescriptions and procedures, health insurance benefits and more.

Scams and Prevention Tips

Scams come in all shapes and sizes. See how identity thieves get creative when it comes to stealing your information, and how to avoid falling for their scams.
