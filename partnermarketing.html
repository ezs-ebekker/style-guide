Partner Marketing - Introduction

EZShield’s Adoption Marketing Campaign gives our partners the freedom to customize a marketing strategy that best fits their unique business goals and needs. EZShield offers multi-channel solutions to help promote identity crime awareness for our partners’ ID Protection programs both online and within their organization.

Adoption Marketing Program Overview

Step 1: Pick Your Theme

Customize how your organization markets your ID Protection program. Choose from our eight themes – whose messages focus on a variety of generational audiences – based on your unique target audience.

Step 2: Pick Your Display

Choose from print and digital display options that best fit your organization’s chosen marketing strategy to be used in your own marketing campaigns.

Step 3: Pick Your Educational Resources & Tools

Choose from any of our additional educational tools that are designed to support your program.

Step 1: Pick Your Theme

Every business is different, but the concept of marketing remains the same: tailored solutions for your audience so that you can better cater to their specific needs. We offer eight themes that speak to various generational groups (i.e. Baby Boomers, Generation X, Millennials, etc.) so you can choose a theme to best fit your program.

Message

Step 2: Pick Your Display

Print Display

Print displays are ready to be used to support your organization’s on-site marketing campaigns. Place pull-up banners and table tents on your counters, and hang small and large posters on your walls to reinforce your ID Protection messaging to anyone stopping by.

Desktop and Mobile Web Ads

Digital displays are available to spread your messaging through online platforms. Opt into desktop or mobile ads so that your reach extends outside of your organization’s doors.

Step 3: Pick Your Educational Resources & Tools

We provide several additional resources that act as educational tools to promote your program. Here are some ways our additional resources can further support your marketing efforts:

  • Use our guide on harmful and misleading language to be sure your messaging is accurate and effective.

  • Place the Live Fraud Feed on your website to keep your customers or employees up-to-date on the latest ID protection tips, breach and scam news updates.

  • Educational content on fraud, identity theft and other identity crime risks by providing informational data sheets and videos.

Educational Data Sheets

Our goal is to empower others to proactively secure their sensitive information and overall identities. Place any of these educational data sheets in your organization or as downloadables on your website to offer valuable information and tips on various types of identity theft, scams and other identity crimes.

Social Media Safety

Learn about the risks posed to your identity when using social media platforms, and how to properly secure your information on these social sites.

Child Identity Theft

Criminal Identity Theft

Synthetic Identity Theft

Find out what makes synthetic identity theft so difficult to catch, and how to spot the signs before it becomes a bigger issue.

Medical Identity Theft

Your identity can be used for more than just stealing funds. Learn how criminals use your identity to acquire medical prescriptions and procedures, health insurance benefits and more.

Scams and Prevention Tips

Scams come in all shapes and sizes. See how identity thieves get creative when it comes to stealing your information, and how to avoid falling for their scams.
