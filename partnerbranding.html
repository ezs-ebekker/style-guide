Level 1: EZShield as the only brand

EZShield is presented as the only brand in your program through web and email.

Level 2: EZShield's brand as the lead, partnering with you

EZShield is presented as clearly partnering with your brand, with EZShield as the lead identity through web and email.

Level 3: Your brand as the lead, seen clearly partnering with EZShield

Lead with your brand, but clearly show your partnership with EZShield through web and email.

Create your own program brand, seen clearly powered by EZShield through web and email.

Level 5: Your branded program

Powered By EZShield Logo Guidelines

Powered By EZShield

For website/email/marketing footers, the full color Powered By EZShield logo should be used.

Standard Full Color, light or dark backgrounds

Follow the guidelines above for the "Powered By EZShield" logo use. The full color version is to be used as the default logo.

Minimum Size

It is not permissible to use the logo less than 100 pixels wide (web) or 1 inch (print) to ensure a crisp legible image.
