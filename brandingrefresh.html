Understanding Branding Levels for Partner Programs

When you implment your program we have 4 levels of branding for you to choose from. Branding for each level is fully implmented in your program from our websites, dashboard and email communications.

Level 1: EZShield as the only brand

EZShield is presented as the only brand in your program through web and email.

Level 2: EZShield as the only brand, limited partner brand visibility

EZShield is presented as the only brand in your program through web and email.

Level 3: EZShield's brand as the lead, partnering with you

EZShield is presented as clearly partnering with your brand, appearing as the lead brand logo through web and email.

Level 4: Your branded program, Powered by EZShield

Create your own program brand, seen clearly Powered by EZShield through web and email.
